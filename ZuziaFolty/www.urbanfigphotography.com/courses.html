Learn Photography

Easy, self-paced photography education no matter your experience level!

Based in Chilliwack, Urban Fig Photography Courses has something for everyone!

FOR THE MOM WITH THE CAMERA PHONE, TO THE EXPERIENCED PHOTOGRAPHER

At Urban Fig Photography, you’ll find an extensive amount of self-paced photography classes that cater to everyone — from parents trying to take better photos of their kids, in person photography workshops for those who are more hands on, and courses for the more advanced photographer to help with business, editing, lighting, and posing. Urban Fig Photography Courses is run by Jete Devisser, an award-winning wedding photographer who has been serving the Fraser Valley since 2010.  Jete has three of her own kids and understands the lack of time that comes with raising a family.  She is excited to help you grow in your understanding of photography and teach you how to take better photos.
