FAQ’s

It’s been too long since we’ve had photos of our family taken!! How do we book?

Just shoot us a message via the contact page, and we’ll find a date that works for your family!

What happens if the weather is awful on the day of our shoot?

We allow one reschedule due to weather, within 3 months of the original date. Sometimes it’s pretty difficult to reschedule family sessions, in which case we’ll suggest areas that have cover!  

What is included in our session?

Family sessions generally take around an hour, and include photos of everyone together, parents together, each parent with the kiddos, and each kiddo individually. When we book your session, we’ll also book your viewing and ordering party which happens a few days after the session itself. Your session fee includes a $400 print credit to put towards any products you want! We’re hugely passionate about creating art for homes — we want you to actually have something tangible from your experience!

What kind of products do you offer?

We can do large canvases or prints, collages, albums or folio boxes — which are a more contemporary “unbound” type of album. We will find out more about you and your home during the process to ensure we’re recommending products that suit your life!

Do you have any suggestions on how to coordinate outfits for our family?

Pinterest is a fabulous resource for outfit combo ideas. Zara and H&M are great stores for coordinating clothes — they often have many different styles within a specific colour palette.

FAQ’s

How many people can be included in an extended family shoot?

Up to 25. If you have more, just shoot us a message and we can give you a specific quote! 

How in the world do we coordinate outfits for a group this size?

We recommend everyone wear neutral colours (such as denim, grey & white), and then choose a couple of additional colours such as burnt orange and light blue. Check out Pinterest for some inspiration and send them to your family members once you decide on a colour scheme.

What is included in our session?

Extended family sessions are generally around 1.5 hours, and include as many different configurations as you wish! When we book your session, we’ll also book your viewing and ordering party which happens a few days after the session itself. Your session fee includes a $600 CAD print credit to put towards any products you want! We’re hugely passionate about creating art for homes — we want you to actually have something tangible from your experience!

What kind of products do you offer?

We can do large canvases or prints, collages, albums or folio boxes — which are a more contemporary “unbound” type of album. We will find out more about you and your home during the process to ensure we’re recommending products that suit your life!

How many people can be present at the viewing party?

We prefer to do individual viewing parties per family — it’s a lot to manage with multiple families ordering at one time! This way we can tailor each appointment specifically to each family and their home.

FAQ’s

When should I book a maternity shoot?

It depends on how you carry! We like to shoot when you have an obvious baby bump – but don’t want to wait too long! Like you, we want ‘glowing’ and ‘serene’ rather than ‘retaining a ton of water’ and ‘feeling like the Pillsbury Doughboy’s little sister’! We suggest around 32-34 weeks of pregnancy, but it varies from mom to mom!

Can my spouse/significant other be a part of the session as well?

Yes! Being parents to this babe is a joint effort! We'll photograph the parents together, as well as mama + bump individually.

What is included in our session?

Maternity sessions are usually around 1 hour, and include location and outfit planning prior to the shoot. When we book your session, we will also book your viewing and ordering party which happens a few days after the session itself. Your session fee includes a $400 print credit to put towards any products you want! We’re hugely passionate about creating art for homes — we want you to actually have something tangible from your experience!

What kind of products do you offer?

We can do large canvases or prints, collages, albums or folio boxes — which are a more contemporary “unbound” type of album. We will find out more about you and your home during the process to ensure we’re recommending products that suit your life!

Do you offer newborn photos as well?

We prefer to leave that in the capable hands of photographers who specialize in newborn photography. It is a niche all on its own!  However, if you are interested in having us come to your home for a lifestyle session with the new baby, we’re more than game to be a part of that!
